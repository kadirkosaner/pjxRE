# Talk Topics – Hangi Konu Hangi Stat Verir?

**Statlar:** `friendship` | `trust` | `love` | `lust`  
**Tier:** friendship < 40 → tier1 | 40–69 → tier2 | 70+ → tier3  
**Ortak:** Her Talk +15 dk, -5 energy. Backyard/Garage’da ek: mood +3/+5.
