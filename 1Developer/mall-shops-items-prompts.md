## Prompt Şablonu (Yeni Item Eklerken)

```
1:1. A young fit woman wearing [GARMENT AÇIKLAMASI]; slight three-quarter angle; framed from [ÇERÇEVE: neck to ribcage / waist to thigh / ankles / vb.], face cropped out; [Kumaş, kesim, detay]; [Aesthetic]. Studio lighting, isolated on pure white background, focus on the garment.
```

- **Tops/Bras:** `framed from neck to ribcage`
- **Bottoms:** `framed from waist to upper thigh` veya `waist to mid-thigh`
- **Dresses:** `framed from chest to knees`
- **Shoes/Socks:** `framed from ankle to [mid-calf/foot]`, `low angle`
- **Jewelry:** `close-up of [ear/wrist/neck]`, `focus on the jewelry`
