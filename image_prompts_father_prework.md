# FATHER - IMAGE PROMPTS (PRE-WORK PHASE)

**Context:** Before the father starts his exhausting job. He is more energetic, hopeful, and present.
**Outfit:** Casual home clothes or neat garage attire. NOT the disheveled business suit (that is for Post-Work).

---

## 1. Topic: Work - New Job (Pre-Work)

_Context: Nervous excitement about the new engineering job._

**Tier 1: The Announcement**

- **Scene:** Looking through paperwork in Living Room.
- **Prompt:** `photorealistic, medium shot back view of father in casual polo shirt and jeans standing in living room holding a stack of papers, reviewing new job contract, posture straight and attentive, daughter (20) with pony tail visible in background, morning light through window, camera behind subject, no face.`
- **Details:** Clean shaven, neat hair, bright lighting.

**Tier 2: Nervousness**

- **Scene:** Drinking coffee in Kitchen, vulnerable moment.
- **Prompt:** `photorealistic, over the shoulder shot from directly behind father sitting at kitchen table, hands wrapped around a coffee mug, gazing thoughtfully into distance, daughter (20) sitting opposite or nearby listening, father wearing comfortable weekend sweater, soft morning lighting, camera behind both subjects, no faces.`

**Tier 3: The Promise**

- **Scene:** Emotional promise in Living Room.
- **Prompt:** `photorealistic, back view of father and daughter (20) standing close in living room, father holding daughter's hands, sincere expression in body language (leaning in), father wearing clean button-down shirt tucked in, warm interaction, bright indoor lighting, camera positioned behind both, no faces.`
